Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone or iPad: open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Pulse → Cancel Subscription. Uninstalling the app does not cancel the subscription.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and request a refund for your Pulse purchase.

How do I delete my account?

Open Pulse and go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Deletion removes your authentication record, clears on-device data (bookmarks, reading history, preferences, cached content), and signs you out. Data in your private iCloud container can be removed via iOS Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage.

Why is the Daily Digest empty or slow to generate?

The AI Daily Digest runs entirely on your device using a local language model. Generation time depends on your device; newer iPhones (A14 chip and later) are significantly faster. Generation runs only when the app is in the foreground and may pause if the device is low on memory. Close background apps and try again.

My bookmarks aren't syncing across devices.

Bookmarks sync via your private iCloud container. Verify:

  • You are signed in to the same iCloud account on both devices.
  • iCloud Drive is enabled in iOS Settings.
  • Pulse has permission to use iCloud (Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Apps using iCloud → Pulse).
  • Both devices are connected to the internet.

Sync may take a few minutes after the first change.

An article won't load, or the summary fails.

Pulse links to articles on the original publisher's site. If the site is down or blocks external viewers, the article may not open. For summaries: AI generation requires sufficient memory and a successfully loaded local model — restart the app if the issue persists.

Notifications aren't arriving.

Open iOS Settings → Notifications → Pulse and confirm notifications are allowed. Inside Pulse, verify notification preferences under Settings.

How do I change the app language?

In-app: Settings → Language. This controls the app UI and the news feed language (English, Portuguese, Spanish).

The app feels slow or the AI features won't start.

The on-device AI model requires approximately 1–1.5 GB of available memory to load. Close background apps, ensure your device has sufficient free storage, and retry. Restarting the device can also help recover memory.

I'm being asked to re-authenticate to delete my account.

This is a security requirement. Sign in again with your original provider (Apple or Google) and try deletion again.
