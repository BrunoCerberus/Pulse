Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026 · Effective: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pulse ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Pulse iOS application (the "Service"). By using Pulse, you agree to this policy.

Summary. Pulse processes your preferences and history on your device and in your private iCloud container. We use third parties (Firebase, Supabase, Apple) to operate the service. We do not sell your data and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account information

When you sign in, we create an account tied to your authentication provider:

  • Sign in with Apple: We receive a private relay identifier and, optionally, your name. We never receive your real Apple ID email unless you choose to share it.
  • Sign in with Google: We receive your Google account email, name, and profile photo URL.

1.2 Data you generate in the app

The following is stored locally on your device and synchronized to your private iCloud database. Only you can access this data; Apple encrypts it and we cannot read it:

  • Bookmarked articles
  • Reading history (articles you opened)
  • App preferences (language, theme, notification settings, topic selections)

To operate the app and diagnose issues, we collect:

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core features: headlines, search, bookmarks, reading history, AI summaries.
  • To synchronize your bookmarks and reading history across your Apple devices using your private iCloud container.
  • To measure aggregate feature usage and improve the app.
  • To diagnose crashes and stability issues.
  • To authenticate you and manage your subscription.

3. On-Device AI Processing

Pulse performs article summarization and "Daily Digest" generation entirely on your device using a locally stored Gemma 3 1B model. Article text processed by the AI never leaves your device. AI-generated summaries may contain inaccuracies; always verify important information against the original source.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained until you delete your account.
  • iCloud data (bookmarks, history, preferences): stored in your personal iCloud container; remove it by deleting your account in-app or by deleting the app and clearing its iCloud data in iOS Settings.
  • Analytics and crash data: retained per Firebase's default retention (up to 14 months for analytics, 90 days for crash reports).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and associated data — available in-app under Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Port your data to another service.
  • Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority (EU/UK residents).
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (California residents) — we do not sell personal information.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the app: Settings → Account → Delete Account. Deletion removes your authentication record, clears on-device data (bookmarks, reading history, preferences, cached content), and signs you out. Data in your private iCloud container is controlled by you and can be removed via iOS Settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Pulse is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum digital-consent age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Our processors operate servers in multiple regions. When data is transferred outside your country, we rely on standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards offered by the processor.

10. Security

We use HTTPS for all network traffic, Apple's Keychain for sensitive credentials, and Apple's encrypted private CloudKit database for synchronized user data. No system is perfectly secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app or applicable law. Material changes will be announced in-app and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
